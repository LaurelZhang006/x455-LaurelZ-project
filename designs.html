Forum of Moral Development Conference Visual Design

This project, in 2022, involved designing the main visual and a set of conference materials for the Forum of Moral Development, organized by the Institute for Moral Development at Southeast University in Nanjing, China. This marked the third consecutive year I was entrusted with the visual design for their conference. The conference theme focused on pressing social issues such as the influence of industrial and high-tech developments, requiring a thoughtful and impactful visual approach. 2020-2024 Yu Yuan Garden Annual Pass Design

This project is one of our long-term collaborations with Yu Yuan Garden, involving the design of an annual park membership card. The product includes sections for Yu Yuan Garden's introduction, four discount coupons, one admission ticket, and the annual membership card. A unified product structure is essential, ensuring physical connections between each section. The challenge lies in creating a design that meets functional requirements while enhancing the user experience through thoughtful design elements and high-quality materials, reflecting the beauty and cultural significance of Yu Yuan Garden.

Design of Promotional Materials for Event

Starting from July 2017, I spearheaded a non-profit initiative focused on multilingual exchange activities in Nanjing, which quickly grew into one of the largest language exchange events in the region. Over the course of two years, I established and led a dedicated team of 20 volunteers, orchestrating a wide range of activities and interactions that fostered cross-cultural communication and understanding. Unfortunately, the event had to be suspended in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Throughout its operation, my responsibilities extended beyond organizing the events; I was actively involved in designing numerous promotional materials to effectively communicate our mission and engage participants. Additionally, I took charge of scripting, conducting interviews, and coordinating on-site activities for video shoots, which were carried out by a team of volunteer photographers. This comprehensive involvement ensured the event's smooth execution and the effective dissemination of our message.

2019-2024 Qinhuai District Library Event Poster Design

The project highlights an extensive range of promotional and exhibition posters created for the Nanjing Qinhuai District Library over a five-year period, from 2019 to 2024. These posters were designed to support various library events, programs, and exhibitions, reflecting the library's commitment to community engagement and cultural enrichment. Each poster was meticulously crafted to capture the essence of the events and exhibitions, using compelling visuals and informative content to attract and inform the public. This body of work demonstrates our dedication to producing high-quality, impactful designs that enhance the library's presence and outreach within the community.
