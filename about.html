About
Visual Storytelling and Project Highlights
I'm Laurel Zhang, a mechanical design graduate who found my calling in graphic design. Since establishing my studio in 2017, I've dedicated myself to translating creative ideas into impactful products for local institutions. My approach blends practicality with cultural sustainability, ensuring each design project resonates beyond its surface. My portfolio includes innovative projects like the multi-functional ticket design for Yu Yuan Garden and the culturally inspired packaging for Qinhuai District Library. By integrating my engineering background with graphic design principles, I ensure that every product not only meets creative objectives but also excels in material feasibility, environmental responsibility, and cost-effectiveness.
In addition to my creative endeavors, I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Graphic Design and User Experience Design. This academic pursuit aims to deepen my understanding of user-centric design principles and digital marketing strategies. I am driven to empower underrepresented sectors with impactful branding and user experience solutions, bridging the gap between creativity and business effectiveness.
