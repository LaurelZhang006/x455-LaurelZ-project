Visual Storytelling and Project Highlights

Starting from July 2017, I spearheaded a non-profit initiative focused on multilingual exchange activities in Nanjing, which quickly grew into one of the largest language exchange events in the region. Over the course of two years, I established and led a dedicated team of 20 volunteers, orchestrating a wide range of activities and interactions that fostered cross-cultural communication and understanding. Unfortunately, the event had to be suspended in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Throughout its operation, my responsibilities extended beyond organizing the events; I was actively involved in designing numerous promotional materials to effectively communicate our mission and engage participants. Additionally, I took charge of scripting, conducting interviews, and coordinating on-site activities for video shoots, which were carried out by a team of volunteer photographers. This comprehensive involvement ensured the event's smooth execution and the effective dissemination of our message.
