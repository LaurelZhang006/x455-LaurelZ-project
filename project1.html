Forum of Moral Development Conference Visual Design
Identity design | Brand guidelines | Content design | Illustration | Product mockups | Packaging artwork | Photography direction | Brochure design
This project, in 2022, involved designing the main visual and a set of conference materials for the Forum of Moral Development, organized by the Institute for Moral Development at Southeast University in Nanjing, China. This marked the third consecutive year I was entrusted with the visual design for their conference. The conference theme focused on pressing social issues such as the influence of industrial and high-tech developments, requiring a thoughtful and impactful visual approach.
